CYBER STRIKE

HUNT THE HACKER

CYBER STRIK: HUNT THE HACKER is an interactive browser-based cybersecurity game designed to build awareness and skills in recognizing and defending against common cyber threats. Players progress through four immersive levels, each simulating a real-world cyberattack scenario.

  • Level 1: CyberMail Defense – Identify phishing emails by analyzing suspicious senders, links, and content.
  • Level 2: Ransom Raid – Detect ransomware through file behavior and stop the attack in time.
  • Level 3: Code Breach – Prevent SQL injection attacks by validating inputs and queries.
  • Level 4: Packet Probe – Analyze network packets to find anomalies and intrusions.

Goal: Empower users with hands-on experience to detect threats, make quick decisions, and build critical thinking in a gamified environment.
